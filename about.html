HymnBook

An application for easy and free access to Baptist hymns.

Overview

This application is a collection of some Baptist hymns developed by Jacob KYAKAKALA. It aims to provide easy and free access to these songs on your mobile device. Whether you’re at home or on the go, you can enjoy the uplifting messages of these hymns without needing a physical book.

Features

  • Offline Access: Read hymns without internet connectivity.
  • Light and Dark theme: Change the theme according to preference, dark or light.
  • Search Hymns: find hymns by typing the title, the number, or any word in the hymn Lyrics.
